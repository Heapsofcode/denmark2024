Lego House

Lego House is a 12,000-square metre building filled with 25 million Lego bricks in Billund, Denmark, located near Legoland and the headquarters of The Lego Group. It is also known as Home of the Brick with reference to Billund, where Lego originates. Visitors can experience a variety of activities during their visit, including physically and digitally building with Lego bricks, programming robots and animating models. The centre's visitor experience includes four experience zones, two exhibitions and the Lego Museum, which showcases the history of the Lego brand and company.

Egeskov Castle

Egeskov is a Danish manor at Kværndrup south of Ringe on South Funen in Denmark . The castle, which is one of Europe's best-preserved moated castles , was built in 1554 by Reichsmarsch Frands Brockenhuus . The farm is older and can be dated to the 15th century . The estate is today 1137 hectares.

Jelling

Jelling is an old and important historical town in the history of Denmark. In the Viking Age it served as the royal seat of the first Monarchs of the Kingdom of Denmark. Jelling is the site of a large stone ship and two large burial mounds, the Jelling stones, and Jelling Church. They have been UNESCO World Heritage Sites since 1994.

Fjordenshus

Fjordenhus is Vejle's iconic building on the harbor. The Danish-Icelandic artist Olafur Eliasson and his studio have designed and is presenting new construction techniques and artworks.

The Wave

The Wave is a housing development by Skyttehusbugten in Vejle, which is inspired by Jørn Utzon's opera house in Sydney and the hilly landscape around Vejle Fjord. Henning Larsen Architects has designed the building.

Vejle Town

Vejle's main pedestrian street is almost a kilometre long. In the pedestrian street, You will find charming shopping streets, including excellent restaurants, trendy outdoor cafes. In Vejle, you will find the perfect harmony between remaining old buildings and new trendy architecture.

Park and Beach

Skyttehushaven is on the northern side of Vejle Fjord. The nearby garden is a popular meeting place for parties and concerts. Here you have a beautiful view of the fjord with the majestic Vejlefjord Bridge and Vejle's harbor front.
